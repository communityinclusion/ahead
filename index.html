Welcome to the Campus Disability Resource (CeDaR) database! Here you can find disability-related information on colleges and universities across the United States.

Start your search by selecting terms in the navigation bar on the left. Use the Advanced Filters to refine your search further. You can also search by key words if there are specific services or features that are important to you such as academic coaching, tutoring, or mentoring.

If you need other assistance or have questions about disability and higher education in the U.S. contact the National Center for College Students with Disabilities at NCCSD@ahead.org.
